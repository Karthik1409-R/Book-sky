Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert Kiyosaki

personal finance book that contrasts the financial philosophies of two father figures—his "Rich Dad" (his friend’s wealthy father) and his "Poor Dad" (his biological father). It emphasizes financial education, investing, and building wealth through assets like real estate and businesses rather than relying solely on a paycheck. The book encourages readers to develop financial intelligence, take risks, and break free from the traditional cycle of working for money.
